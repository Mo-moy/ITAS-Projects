About the Game

Gallop and Deliver invites players into a thrilling world of high-stakes equestrian racing set in the vibrant and treacherous landscapes of early Renaissance Europe. In this fast-paced arcade-style game, you’ll assume the role of a daring courier tasked with delivering illegal luggage through bustling towns, winding country roads, and majestic castles.

As you race against time and rival couriers, you must navigate challenging terrains, dodge the authorities, and outsmart competitors. Choose from a variety of uniquely skilled horses, each with its own strengths and abilities, and customize your gear for speed, agility, and stealth.

Compete in exhilarating races across iconic European cities, from the cobblestone streets of Florence to the enchanting forests of Germany. Unlock new routes, upgrade your steeds, and collect power-ups to gain an edge over your opponents. But beware: the authorities are hot on your heels, and one wrong move could land you in trouble!

Will you become the fastest and most notorious courier in Renaissance Europe, or will you be caught in the act? Saddle up and get ready for the ride of your life in Gallop and Deliver!
